These scoring rules are fixed to the official World Triathlon Ranking criteria and can't be changed here: points decrease 7.5% per position, the best 6 results from the trailing 52 weeks count at full value, and the best 6 results from the prior 53–104 weeks count at 1/3 value. The Top-5 bonus is applied automatically for Continental Triathlon Championships (e.g. "Europe/Americas/Africa/Oceania/Asia Triathlon Championships") — it's not user-editable. See the full World Triathlon Ranking document for details. Quality of Field Factor and per-event caps are not auto-applied; adjust "Points Available" manually if you know the QFF-adjusted figure.
